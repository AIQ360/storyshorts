## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Do I need cPanel or traditional hosting?">
    No. Framecast AI is cloud native, meaning you only need a domain (and even that is optional). With platforms like **Vercel**, **Supabase**, and **Resend**, you can deploy in minutes without server maintenance. Their generous free tiers let you start generating revenue before incurring any costs.
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use a different email provider instead of Resend?">
  Absolutely. Resend is pre-configured for simplicity, but you can swap in any
  email service. Just replace the API key and sender email address in your
  environment variables.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Framecast AI free to use?">
  The Framecast AI codebase is available for purchase. Running it involves
  minimal costs: Astria's API for AI model training starts at just **$0.10 for 8
  headshots**. Hosting on Vercel, Supabase, and Resend all offer generous free
  tiers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment gateways are supported?">
  Framecast AI ships with three payment gateways out of the box: **Stripe**,
  **PayPal**, and **Razorpay**. You can enable one or all of them depending on
  your target audience and region.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are the ongoing costs after purchase?">
  Like any SaaS business, there are operational costs for third-party services
  (Astria, Vercel, Supabase, Resend). However, all of these platforms offer free
  tiers that can support your first few thousand users. As your revenue grows,
  these costs scale proportionally.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are platforms like Vercel and Supabase expensive at scale?">
  Not at all. Vercel offers unlimited project hosting for free. Resend provides
  3,000 emails per month at no cost. Supabase is open source with a generous
  free tier. These are production grade services used by companies worldwide and
  far more cost effective than traditional hosting.
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm not a developer. Can I still use this?">
    Framecast AI is designed to be developer friendly with comprehensive documentation. If you can follow step by step guides, you can deploy it. For marketing, organic social media content is your best bet as short form video content can drive massive user growth.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
