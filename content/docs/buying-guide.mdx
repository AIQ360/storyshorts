## Which License To Buy?

Framecast AI offers two license types depending on how you plan to use it:

<Cards>
  <Card
    title="Regular License"
    description="For personal or internal use projects where end users are not charged."
  />
  <Card
    title="Extended License"
    description="For commercial projects, SaaS platforms, paid services, and revenue generating products."
  />
</Cards>

### Regular License

The Regular License is ideal if you are building something for personal use, a portfolio piece, or a client project where there is no direct revenue generation from end users.

What this covers:

- One time purchase for a single project
- No ongoing licensing costs
- Full access to every feature in the codebase
- Suitable for personal projects, internal tools, or any product where users are not paying to access it

<Callout>
  If you want to explore the platform, build something for yourself, or use it
  for a client without charging their users, the Regular License is the right
  choice.
</Callout>

### Extended License

The Extended License is built for professionals and businesses who want to turn Framecast AI into a commercial product.

What this covers:

- Full rights to charge end users for access to the product you build
- Use Framecast AI in one paid, scalable, and revenue generating project
- No restrictions on how much you charge your users
- Perfect for SaaS platforms, membership sites, and any product where monetization is the goal

<Callout>
  If you plan to generate revenue from the product you build with Framecast AI,
  the Extended License is required.
</Callout>
